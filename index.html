    I am translating a Japanese novel "Yabunonaka (In the bush)" (1922) written by Ryunosuke Akutagawa (1892-1927) into Lojban. This is the original story of the film "Rashomon" (1950) of Akira Kurosawa (1910-1998).

    The copyright of the original text expired. It is downloadable at Aozora Bunko.
